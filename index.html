Our Story

A boutique sanctuary in the Doon Valley

Chitralekha Boutique Resort is crafted for travellers who crave intimacy without compromising on comfort. Perched where Dehradun meets the sky, our property blends contemporary design with the soul of the Himalayas.

Wake to valley mist rolling past your window. Spend afternoons by the pool or on your private lawn. Evenings unfold at Buransh Blossoms — our restaurant celebrating local flavours and continental finesse.

  • Mussoorie & jungle panoramas
  • Minutes from Robber's Cave & Malsi Deer Park
  • Weddings & celebrations welcome
